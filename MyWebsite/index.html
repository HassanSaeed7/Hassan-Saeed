About

Hello! Thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio. I am Hassan Saeed and I am looking forward to meeting you! A little bit about me, I am a recent graduate from the University of California, Riverside. While I was majoring in public policy, I took an intro to CS course to fulfill an elective; I wish I had taken it much sooner because I had never been that excited about staying up until 2 am to complete homework. There is just something about writing a working algorithm for the first time. I'm sure whoever is reading this is very familiar with the feeling. Ever since, I have been deeply fascinated with computer programming. By the time I had realized how passionate I felt towards the subject, it was far too late to switch majors. Regardless, I felt like I could not stop my learning with this one course so I kept doing tutorials in my free time. These code camps maintained a vice grip on my attention like nothing else ever has, like I was a cat focused on a laser pointer. I felt like I was not getting very far with them until I began making websites. I am interested in web development and have been developing my front-end skills through code camps. I enjoy creating sites but am interested in developing all skills pertaining to the deployment and integration of websites and web applications. I am hoping to become a full stack developer but am now trying to break into the field with front-end development. I am familiar with:

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript SCSS/SASS React.JS WordPress GIT
